Hi there

Rosie

Hello everyone, my name is Rosie. I am happy to be chosen by people to convey meaningful moments through photographs. Since I was a child I had a special passion for photography, I often followed older brothers and sisters to watch them take pictures. After growing up I studied economics but still didn’t give up my passion, then I took a basic photography class and since I have a bit of talent, I developed quite well. As a result, I perfected my brainchild, Rosie Studio. For me, every photo is a moment and a memory that cannot be replaced. Therefore, hope that when you are here to collaborate with me, we will create the most beautiful and wonderful memories.
